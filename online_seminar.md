# Seminar #67 - Tidy Data, Wide Data & Long Data
## Date/Time: 5/4/2022, Noon ET, 9AM PT, 5PM GMT, 6PM CET
## Duration: 1 hour
## Meeting URL: [https://zoom.us/j/331585134](https://us02web.zoom.us/j/331585134?pwd=VGVyeXBRWjFMT2hESFdhSU45Z2d0dz09)

Agenda:

Often, you encounter a problem where you have data but you can't easily visualize, create calculations, or analyze. This is a very common data problems and it happens because the data is not in the right format for your job.

This is why it's important to understand 'Tidy Data' concept and have a few techniques in your toolbox to transfer data between Wide and Long data formats in a flexible way.

In this seminar, Kan will introduce the Tidy Data concept and show you how you can solve the data format problems quickly so that you can visualize and analyze more data.
